About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at South China University of Technology. I am passionate about exploring cutting-edge technologies and dedicated to developing innovative solutions for real-world challenges. Currently, I focus on developing intelligent systems and innovative platforms, aiming to advance technology and improve the quality of human life.

I believe in the power of interdisciplinary research and open collaboration. My approach combines rigorous engineering principles with creative problem-solving, always seeking to bridge the gap between theoretical advances and practical applications.
